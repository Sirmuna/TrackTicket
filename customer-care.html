You can cancel your ticket up to 2 hours before departure for a full refund. Cancellations made less than 2 hours before departure are subject to a 50% cancellation fee.
Can I reschedule my journey? ‚ñº
Yes, you can reschedule your journey up to 4 hours before departure. A rescheduling fee of ‚Ç¶500 applies. Subject to seat availability on the new date.
What documents do I need to travel? ‚ñº
You need a valid government-issued ID (National ID, Driver's License, or International Passport) and your booking confirmation (printed or digital).
How much luggage can I bring? ‚ñº
Economy class passengers are allowed 20kg of luggage, Business class 25kg, and First Class 30kg. Additional luggage is charged at ‚Ç¶500 per kilogram.
